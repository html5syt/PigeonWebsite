Phigros is a laneless, multi-judgement-line music game. Originated from Bilibili, music game lovers from all over the world made up "Pigeon Games", making Phigros possible. Phigros features a truly novel gameplay and a curated storyline, all to your delight and awaits your fingertips!

Game Preview

The dynamic judge line dances with the rhythm of music, beating, shifting, moving around, and stacking notes... Rapid reaction is essential during play, so challenge yourself with rhythm and melody! Beautiful charts, enjoyable melody, and 4 difficulties of "EZ", "HD", "IN", and "AT", bring you the delight of playing music while exploring the story hidden beneath...
